16-track fixed deck

Every session opens on a full 16-track layout with hardware-like channel strips, meters, and direct transport feedback.

Always-destructive record

Record behaves like tape: punch, overwrite, and commit. No hidden comp stacks. No indecision loops.

Tape character controls

Shape tone with wow, flutter, saturation, tape age, and damage controls while keeping the core tape workflow fast and tactile.

Workflow

Built for momentum, not menu diving.

TAPE 16 keeps the center of gravity on transport, reels, meters, and commitment. The UI focuses on speed: arm, play, record, repeat.

  • Hardware-style REW, FF, STOP, PLAY, REC controls
  • Track-level arm / solo / mute with clear lamp states
  • Tape character and calibration controls
  • Offline and realtime bounce modes
